### 📈 Marketing Campaign ROI & Performance Analysis
---

## Project Overview (include a link at the top for quick dashboard access)

This project transforms clean marketing data into a strategic decision-making engine. Built for a multi-entity entrepreneur, I developed an analytical ecosystem that moves beyond static reporting to allow for real-time budget simulation. By leveraging Advanced SQL, Window Functions, and DAX logic, I empowered stakeholders to identify high-performing customer segments and project the impact of budget reallocations across a diverse portfolio of companies.

## 🧠 Business Stakeholder Questions Addressed
* If we increase our Acquisition Cost by X%, what is the projected impact on our total ROI?
* Who are the top 3 Customer Segments for each Company by ROI?
* Which channels drive high Impressions but failing to deliver ROI?
* Are campaigns losing effectiveness over time (Duration) vs. previous month benchmarks?
* Which Target Audience + Campaign Type combo yields the lowest CAC with highest Engagement?
* How does ROI vary across different Locations and Languages for the same Campaign Type?

## Dashboard Architecture & UX Strategy (plan)

I designed a two-tier reporting system to ensure the data is actionable for both high-level strategy and daily operations:

Page 1: Portfolio Executive Overview (can edit/check)

* User: The Entrepreneur / Business Owner.
* Strategic Goal: High-level "health check" across all companies.
* Key Feature: What-If Budget Simulation. I implemented a parameter-driven model that allows the owner to project ROI based on hypothetical increases in acquisition spend.

Page 2: Campaign Performance Deep Dive (can edit/check)

* User: Marketing Managers / Channel Leads.
* Strategic Goal: Root-cause analysis and tactical optimization.
* Key Feature: Cross-Report Drill-Through. Users can right-click a company on Page 1 to land on this page, automatically filtered to that company's specific campaign logs, segments, and trend lines.
